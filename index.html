MemoNova – Privacy Policy

MemoNova does not collect, store or share any personal user data.

The game stores highscores and settings locally on your device only.

MemoNova does not:

  • require account registration
  • use external servers
  • track users
  • collect location data
  • collect personal information
  • use analytics services

Audio files and game assets are stored locally within the app.
